About Us

A Legacy of Learning & Leadership

Founded in 1998, Oxford Patils Academy has been a beacon of quality education, nurturing students to become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals ready for tomorrow's world.

🎯

Mission-Driven Education

Every program is designed around academic excellence combined with holistic development.

🔬

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Modern labs, digital classrooms, a 50,000-book library, and sports complex on a 15-acre campus.

🌍

NEET · JEE · PGCET Coaching

Long-term competitive exam coaching alongside academics from Grade 1 through Grade 12.
