Medical & Engineering Aspirants

Founded in 1995, **Oxford Patil's Group of Institutions** has established itself as the premier destination for quality education in North Karnataka. Strategically located in Nagarabetta, Muddebihal, our institution serves as a vital bridge for rural talent to access world-class academic resources.

01

Integrated PU Science Wing

We offer a rigorous PCMB/PCMC curriculum that perfectly balances Karnataka State Board requirements with deep-dive coaching for **NEET, JEE, and KCET**.

02

Residential Mentorship Program

Our hostel facilities are designed to provide a "Gurukul" environment—safe, disciplined, and free from distractions, ensuring 24/7 academic support.

03

Modern Infrastructure

From high-tech science laboratories to a digitally-enabled library, we provide the tools necessary for modern competitive exam preparation.
